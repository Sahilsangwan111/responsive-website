Our Story

Founded in 2005 by Mr. Rajesh Sangwan, Sangwan Medical Shop began as a small neighborhood pharmacy with a vision to provide authentic medicines at fair prices. What started as a single counter has grown into one of the most trusted medical stores in West Delhi, serving thousands of families.

We take pride in our knowledgeable staff who undergo regular training to stay updated on new medicines and healthcare products. Our pharmacists are always available to answer your questions and provide guidance on medication usage.

Over the years, we've built strong relationships with our customers who trust us for their family's healthcare needs. Many of our original customers from 2005 still visit us today, and we've had the privilege of serving multiple generations of families.
