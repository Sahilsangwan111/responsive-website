About Us

Sangwan Medical Shop has been serving the local community with genuine medicines and healthcare products since 2005. Our founder, Mr. Rajesh Sangwan, started this pharmacy with a vision to provide affordable and authentic medicines to everyone in our neighborhood.

We take pride in our knowledgeable staff who can guide you about medicines and their proper usage. Our shop maintains proper storage conditions for all medicines as per regulatory requirements.

Over the years, we've built trust with thousands of customers who rely on us for their medical needs. Whether you need regular medicines or emergency supplies, we're here to help.
